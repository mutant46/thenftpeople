introduction

Space Troopers is an art and gaming project, with utility. We are offering 3d modelled art, each individually rendered and all unique from one another, highly detailed and stylised, with original traits and some inspired by classic sci-fi. We are aiming for our art to be good enough to be considered an art project outright, but we are also providing reasons to hold your Space Trooper by adding returns and utility. Some of our NFTs will offer passive income, Ada sent straight to the wallet containing them from a portion of secondary sales. In addition we will also be offering a chance to enter your Space Trooper in daily battle royale combat via our website! They will earn medals over the week and at the end of the week, the four with the highest (and one with the lowest) medals will be airdropped Ada.

Collection

Space Troopers is a family-run play to earn Cardano NFT project providing a sci-fi gladiator management game enabling players to earn weekly $Ada in arena rewards.

future plan

In addition to game development our immediate plan after mint is to start work on an airdrop for holders, to allow 'destiny choices' - a mechanic which will allow you to send your NFT on missions to evolve them. The decisions made by the holders in destiny choices will shape what happens in the Space Troopers universe and therefore what Season 2 looks like.
